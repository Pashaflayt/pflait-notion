## 💫 **What I Am Looking For**: 


I am seeking a DevOps / Platform / Ops Engineer role bridging system administration, CI/CD, and IT management focused on tool development and architecture design. 
I enjoy teamwork and a startup-style environment. Beyond core responsibilities, I actively help with a company’s media as a technical expert or producer. I have skills in video production and audio engineering. Remote work is preferred; a hybrid format (several days per week) is also acceptable. I am available for on-call rotations. For hybrid work, a well-equipped and comfortable office environment is important for productivity. I love working with motivated people who are care about their work and driven to make the world a better place.


## **Case Studies & Skills**


### **Infrastructure & Virtualization**

- **Hardware:** I set up and maintain HPE, Dell, and Huawei (OceanStor All-Flash) servers. I also handle Synology storage and fiber switching between nodes.
- **Virtualization:** Deep experience with **VMware, Hyper-V, and Proxmox**. I take care of the whole cycle: from design and part sourcing to mounting and daily maintenance.
- **Server Room:** I build racks from scratch, calculate power/cooling needs, and design backup power plans.

**Result:** I can design and build a reliable data center from the ground up.


### **Network & Identity (SSO)**

- **Access:** I set up secure corporate networks using RADIUS, TLS, and IdP integrations.
- **Wi-Fi:** Built UniFi mesh systems so employees have **one account** for both email and Wi-Fi.
- **Self-hosted Wiki:** Deployed **Outline** on Proxmox/Fedora with a modern stack (Docker, MinIO, Redis, Caddy). It’s fully automated via private Gitea and uses Google OIDC for easy login.

### **Cost Optimization & Backups**

- **Smart Backups:** Switched to btrfs snapshots and Proxmox Backup Server.
- **Cutting Costs:** I dive deep into billing reports to find errors or waste. By consolidating services, changing plans, and negotiating discounts with vendors, **I saved the company $9,000/year** without losing any features.

### **Observability & CI/CD**

- **Monitoring:** I use Prometheus and **write custom exporters in Go** (e.g., for Cloudflare Stream) to track the metrics that actually matter for the business.
- **Jenkins Pipelines:** I fix bottlenecks to make builds faster. I keep things secure by using "distroless" images, policy-as-code, and smart secret management. I also automated Cloudflare cache purging to keep content fresh.

### **Internal Tools & Automation**

- **Back-office Support:** I help accounting teams with digital signatures, EDI, and software updates. I act as a bridge between finance, 1C-developers, and DBAs.
- **Custom Scripts:** I love automating routine tasks. For example, I wrote JS scripts for **YouTrack** to auto-import Release Notes from GitHub and created cleanup tools to keep cloud databases small and easy to back up.
